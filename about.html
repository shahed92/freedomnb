about freedomnb , inc

Freedomnb Transportation was established with the purpose of providing a safe and convenient means of transportation to individuals who are aging, disabled, or have limited mobility. Our company is dedicated to serving clients of all ages and with different conditions.

We strive to make a difference in our community through our non-emergency medical transportation services. Our staff members see to it that each individual we serve is treated with the utmost respect. We value the trust our clients have placed in us, and we’re committed to helping your aging and disabled loved ones go to their various appointments comfortably and hassle-free.

For more information about our company, please feel free to contact us.
